Oslo Metropolitan University, Norway

Detailed information via the university website

EPS is offered at Oslo and Akershus Uni­versity College of Applied Sci­ences
in the city center of Oslo, very close to the National Theatre.
Stu­dents fly into Oslo’s main airport — Gar­dermoen
and take either the high speed train or bus to the city center.

Check out their easily nav­i­gable website.
Appli­cation infor­mation and other per­tinent infor­mation
are on the links on the right of the page.
Print out the appli­cation form, com­plete it,
and send the form to the address listed on the appli­cation form.
